What is Life Science NEXTGEN?

A new network for the next generation of life science talent.

Life Science NEXTGEN connects students, early-career professionals, leaders and professional communities across the Danish life science ecosystem, making it easier for new talent to find guidance, relationships and opportunities.

Why it matters

Connections that make career paths easier to see.

Access inspiration, leadership insight and professional networks.

Who is it for?

Built for people moving into Danish life science.

  • Students in late Bachelor or Master programs
  • PhD students and researchers
  • Early-career professionals
  • Mid-stage professionals entering leadership or specialisation

Annual Flagship Event

Connecting the Future of Life Science.

The annual flagship event is designed to inspire, connect and support the next generation of life science professionals, while giving today's leaders direct access to a diverse pool of emerging talent.

  • Inspirational keynote speakers and industry perspectives
  • Interactive workshops and active panel discussions
  • Networking opportunities and relationship building
  • Career and leadership insights from across the ecosystem
